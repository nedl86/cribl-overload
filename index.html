HOW TO PLAY
The basics
🗑️
Tap Dismiss for safe events.
🔍
Tap Investigate for threats.
Card colours
Green — always safe. Dismiss it.
Red — always a threat. Investigate it.
Purple — can be either. Check the icon carefully.
Lives & scoring
💔
Lose a life if a threat reaches the bottom, or if you dismiss a threat by mistake.
⚠️
Point deduction if you investigate a safe event — false alarms waste time.
🤦‍♂️
Safe events that reach the bottom score 0 pts — no life lost, but also no points gained. What a waste!
🤖
Tap the gold card directly to collect an AI Agent — once activated, it will automatically sort cards for you.
